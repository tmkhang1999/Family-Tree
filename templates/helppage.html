Sapling - Instructions

Getting Started

Click the "Create New Tree" box in your forest menu and give it a name. From here, you can add members and relations to your tree.

Editing and Deleting a Tree

Click on any of the trees in your forest to edit them. If you want to delete a tree, you can do so by selecting the "Delete" option from the dropdown menu of your tree. But remember, once it's gone, you can't get it back.

Creating, editing and deleting Members

Click the "Create Member" icon at the top right of the interface. You have to give your member a name, but everything else can be filled in later. Once you've hit save, it will appear wherever you click within the canvas. It won't be connected to your other members yet, but you can change that by adding a relation.

Click on your member and its details will pop up to your right. Make any changes you want in these fields and hit save.

If your member is no longer needed, click on it and hit the delete button. Any relations connected to your member will disappear with the member, so delete carefully.

Creating, editing and deleting Relations

Click the "Create Relation" button at the top right of the interface. Then click the first member of your relation, followed by the second. When your relation is created, it will appear in your tree as a solid black line, but you can change that later.

Click on your relation and drag it around until it fits in with the rest of your tree and looks how you want it to. Doing this won't shift existing members and relations, so don't let that stop you while you customize. [how to toggle junction and lined/dashed?]

Adding and Removing Collaborators

If you want some help nurturing your tree, add a collaborator(s) by [...add collab desc...]. If you are the creator of a tree, only you have the right to add or remove collaborators or delete the tree. Everything else (creating, editing, and deleting members and relations) is fair game to both you and your collaborators. If you are the collaborator of a tree, it will appear in your forest like any other tree [...except?], but you won't be able to delete it or add or remove other collaborators.
